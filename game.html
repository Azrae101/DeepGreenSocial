How to Play

1
Drag and drop the falling items into the correct bins below.
2
Sort correctly:
  • Glass: Glass bottles, jars, glass shards
  • Hazardous waste: Batteries, light bulbs, paint
  • Food waste: Food scraps, coffee filters, eggshells
  • Metal: Cans, metal lids, screws
  • Cardboard: Cardboard boxes, egg cartons
  • Paper: Newspapers, office paper, envelopes
  • Plastic: Plastic bottles, trays, bags
  • General waste: Dirty packaging, diapers, cigarette butts
3
Earn points: +1 point for each correct sort.
Avoid mistakes: Lose 1 life for each wrong sort.
