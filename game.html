Trash Sorting Challenge

Test your recycling knowledge by sorting items into the correct bins before they hit the ground!

How to Play:

  • Drag the falling items to the correct bins: Blue for Recycling, Brown for Compost, Black for Trash
  • You have 3 lives - you lose a life if an item hits the ground or goes in the wrong bin
  • Earn 10 points for each correctly sorted item
  • The game gets faster as your score increases
  • Try to beat your high score!
