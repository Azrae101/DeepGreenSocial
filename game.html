How to Play

1
Drag and drop the falling items into the correct bins below.
2
Sort correctly:
  • Blue bin: Paper, cardboard, glass, metal
  • Brown bin: Food waste, compostable items
  • Red bin: Plastic packaging, bottles
  • Black bin: Everything else, non-recyclables
3
Earn points: +1 point for each correct sort.
Avoid mistakes: Lose 1 life for each wrong sort.
4
Game gets faster: As your score increases, items fall faster!
